About Mitch's Surf Shop

Welcome to Mitch’s Surf Shop offering California's most innovative hand shaped surfboards since 1967. We are your water sports specialists with 2 stores in the San Diego area: La Jolla and Solana Beach.

Mitch's crew is always glad to help!
