Shipping to Coastal California

We pack and ship your board on the California Coast for $95.00 for boards 7' and under, $115.00 for boards 7' 1" to 12'. You can pick up your board in the following cities:

  • Orange County
  • Santa Cruz
  • Morro Bay
  • Santa Barbara
  • San Francisco
  • Monterey
  • San Luis Obispo
  • Los Angeles
  • Half Moon Bay
  • Cayucos
  • Pismo Beach

Deliveries north of San Francisco and California inland rates vary, and have to use more expensive packing costs; call or email us for a quote to your location.

Shipping to US

Mitch’s Surf Shop only charges you for the surfboard & packing costs; you will pay the surfboard shipper directly. Most orders arrive within one week. Your board can be shipped in one of two ways; to a home (more expensive) or to a warehouse close to you. (less expensive)
