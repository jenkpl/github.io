Product details

Mandala's new HUNTER SEEKER.

Semi-opaque Deep Wine Red in a sanded gloss finish. Finish by Moonlight Glassing Company of California, USA. Black logo and hand drawn Mandala on deck. Single baswood wedge stringer, 3/16"-1/16" with brown gluelines. Diamond fin patch, single center box with fin included.

The HUNTER SEEKER is a Tri-plane Hull drawn out to a Glider shape. Tear-drop hull bottom with each side panel Tri-plane blending into vee towards the pintail. Cuts through water like a hot knife through butter.

Hand shaped and signed polyurethane foam by Manny Caro.
